I’ve now seen Jessie J’s more recent “Do It Like A Dude” and the sentiment is definitely the same. But Jessie doesn’t emulate male dress in the video, and the “dude like” dancing definitely has as much winding and grinding in it as possible. Which is fine, except that she doesn’t look at all like she’s doing it like a dude. Her backing dancers play more games with the body language and clothes than she actually does. So it’s an odd one – I think the video sort of doesn’t really know what it wants.

Here’s the vid for the interested!

Mind you, on the other side of the coin, it’s got the following:

* women starting fights in a non-prurient way
* she has some amazing lip make up going on in some of the shots – spiky black rhinestones. She’s definitely rocking an aggressively spiky femininity in the vid, even if she’s not doing the same thing Ciara’s done
* there are these shots of hocks of meat, which I assume is some sort of knowing comment…
