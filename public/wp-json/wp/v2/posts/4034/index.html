Dear darling BadRep readers, I’m not happy.\u00a0 Let me avail your face of the reason why.<\/p>\n

<\/a>If you live in Britain, you’ll have noticed that you’ve had a census posted to you.\u00a0 As you should all be aware (but don’t worry if you’re not – I didn’t know about it until this year!) the census <\/a>takes stock of all the citizens of the British Isles, their means, whats and wherefores, and then the government looks at it and goes, “Hmm! Based on the fact that we have this<\/em> many of these<\/em> people, we’ll spend our money this<\/em> way.”<\/p>\n

So it’s actually quite important that the data gathered is accurate.<\/p>\n

Imagine my disgust, then, when I turned to page whatever-it-is where it asked me:<\/p>\n

What is your sex?<\/strong>
\n[ ] Male [ ] Female<\/strong><\/p>\n

You read that right. \u00a0 There’s just<\/em> the box to state your “sex”, and there’s only two boxes, and there’s no mention whatsoever of gender anywhere.<\/p>\n

Now, anyone capable of thought will realise that sex is distinct and non-prescriptive of gender, and gender is not binary. <\/strong> Indeed, neither are binary<\/strong>.\u00a0 For the census to merely ask the (binary) “sex” of the British people is to erase the identities of the numerous trans* and Intersex Brits.\u00a0 What the fuck are we meant to put?\u00a0 Do we tick both? \u00a0 Do we add another box? \u00a0 Do we just shun the whole thing altogether?\u00a0 If there’s a define-own space for religion, why not for gender?<\/p>\n

How are we ever meant to glean accurate data on how many trans* and Intersex people we have living here in the British Isles if there’s no room for them on the bloody census?!<\/p>\n
