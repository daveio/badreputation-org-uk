READERS. <\/p>\n

You have been emailing us! Demanding to know where the linkposts have gone! AND RIGHTLY SO. <\/p>\n

    \n
  • So, to kick off, Slutwalk London comes to the capital!<\/a> And the date has been changed to June 11th<\/strong>! Previously on Found Feminism when it marched through Birmingham, now it’s blown up majorly. Oars of all colours have been stuck in. Us? We’ll be there. If you spot us (we’re contemplating a banner), come say hello. We’re a friendly bunch, promise. <\/li>\n
  • Also on June the 11th, Ladyfest Essex<\/a> – a one day celebration of women and creativity, with the proceeds going to Refuge. Go to Slutwalk, and then head out to Southend!<\/li>\n
  • The lovely people at For Books’ Sake will be at Bromley Literary Festival on the 3rd of July<\/a>. Go along and say hello!<\/li>\n
  • The also-lovely Maegan from TaraBooks, which we are great fans of talks to the Guardian<\/a> and says things that are well worth reading.<\/a><\/li>\n
  • TreasuryIslands<\/a> is a badass blog. (You will already know this if you were reading us a couple of days ago, as writer Libby was guestposting. Scroll down. Read that too. It’s Friday.) Particular favourites: Libby versus teen vampire fiction<\/a> and the posts on feminist-friendly picture books aimed at boys and at girls.<\/li>\n
  • Comics writer Kelly-Sue DeConnick is on a mission<\/a>, and needs your support. Go sponsor her as she raises money for Women for Women International to help women in the Congo.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Have a great weekend!<\/p>\n
