Hello! Steve B<\/strong> here, filling in for our Ed this Friday, as she’s surrounded by majestically-towering amounts of illustration for the upcoming New Cross Turn Left<\/a>.<\/p>\n

You may have noticed that we like comics, art and animation here at BadRep Towers, so some of this week’s links were apparently made JUST FOR US:<\/p>\n

\n<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Firstly a great piece on Japan’s Studio Ghibli<\/a>, and how they’ve had real, heroic female characters for years, making deep art while Disney are still playing catch-up. (Some of BadRep argue about whether ‘Princess Mononoke’ or ‘Spirited Away’ is the better film. Everyone loves Totoro, of course.)\n
  • New webcomic Lady Sabre and the Pirates of the Ineffable Aether<\/a>! It’s Lady Space Pirates. Seriously. They couldn’t have written anything more BadRep if they’d included flying space sharks<\/em> (which they might still do, given the setting!) The comic has just launched, but really – how you can not love something when the poster looks like this?<\/a>\n
  • Artist Lauren Redniss<\/a> has created a truly beautiful book on the life of the first woman to win the Nobel Prize (and then another Nobel Prize), Marie Curie<\/a>. Not just that, but she’s developed each page using Cyanotype<\/a> – the process for making blueprints. Amazing work of art on an equally amazing scientist!<\/li>\n
  • The trailer for Pixar’s next movie<\/a> got us very excited, and Amanda over at TheMarySue<\/a> agrees – she’s listed 20 reasons to be excited about “Brave”<\/a>.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
