    \n
  • SF author John Scalzi<\/strong> has written a beautiful post about Things I Don’t Have To Think About Today.
    \n<\/a><\/li>\n
  • This may be a few months old, but here’s a still-very-readable article by Tiffany Daniels <\/strong>on Women in Music in 2010<\/a> with lots of snazzy recommendations.\u00a0 Its parent zine, God Is In The TV<\/strong>, are hoping it’ll scoop a Record Of The Day Best Music Feature award<\/a>, and if you dig it you can vote for it.\u00a0 And if you like that, there’s more music blogging from Tiffany over at Drunken Werewolf<\/a>.<\/li>\n

  • Peter Tennant writing for Black Static<\/strong> magazine breaks down the statistics regarding women’s writing in recent horror anthologies.<\/a> Horror author Maura McHugh<\/strong> discusses the findings at her blog.<\/a><\/li>\n
