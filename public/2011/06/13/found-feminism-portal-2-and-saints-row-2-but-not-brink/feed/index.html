I loved Saint’s Row 2 – I especially liked how they recorded different VO/cutscenes depending on your gender, without ever making the female avatar less hardcore (as far as I could tell.) I changed genders toward the end of a game, and I loved that the other characters noticed. I also liked the little nod to character customization at the beginning; you meet Johnny Gat, your #2, and his reaction is “You look different… did you get a haircut?”

Saints Row 3 is coming out soon, and I’m just hoping that it deals with gender half as well.

I was bought Brink before I knew that it wouldn’t be including female characters, and I was bitterly disappointed when the news broke. The game is really quite good, but the failure to include women makes me disappointed the entire time that I play. It ties in with my constant disappointment in Team Fortress 2’s lack of gender diversity – which bothered me less before they started doubling down with the “Mann Co” store, further reinforcing the “men only” messages that the game is full of.
